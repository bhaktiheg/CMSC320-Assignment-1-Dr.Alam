

Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git is a great resource for collaboration as it allows teams to collaborate
efficiently by tracking changes, preventing overwrites, and supporting branches for
development and bug fixes.



Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: One field that data science can be applied to is healthcare. By analyzing 
patient data, for example, data scientists can predict disease outbreaks, personalize 
treatment plans, and overall, improve patient outcomes. This ultimately helps in making 
more informed medical decisions and optimizing healthcare resources. 



Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branches by creating a new commit that brings the changes together, 
preserving the history of both brances. Rebasing, on the other hand, moves or reapplies commits from one 
branch to another, creating a linear history by eliminating the divergence between branches.
